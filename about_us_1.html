About The metropolitan

Frontages directly onto Paris Commune Square and Dong Khoi Street position The Metropolitan on one of HCMC’s primary traffic arteries, spelling maximum year-round profitability for the retail outlets. The retail design concept is highly adaptive, accommodating up to four distinct retailers, providing tenants with considerable freedom to create their own identities. Elegant copper-toned steelwork and superb granite finishes provide complementary accompaniments to the expansive full-vision display windows, maximizing retailing effectiveness.
